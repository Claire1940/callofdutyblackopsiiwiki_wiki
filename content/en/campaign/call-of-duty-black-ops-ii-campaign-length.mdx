| Playstyle | Average Time | Key Focus |

| Mission Name | Era | Playable Character | Core Objective |

| **Pyrrhic Victory** | 1986 | Alex Mason | Rescue Frank Woods in Angola alongside Savimbi. |
| **Celerium** | 2025 | David Mason | Infiltrate a research facility in Myanmar for a quantum device. |
| **Old Wounds** | 1986 | Alex Mason | Fight Soviet forces on horseback in Afghanistan. |
| **Time and Fate** | 1986 | Raul Menendez / Mason | Experience the raid on Menendez's compound in Nicaragua. |
| **Fallen Angel** | 2025 | David Mason | Record Menendez's secret meeting in flooded Pakistan. |
| **Karma** | 2025 | David Mason | Infiltrate a floating resort to rescue Chloe Lynch. |
| **Suffer with Me** | 1989 | Frank Woods | Capture Noriega in Panama and survive Menendez's trap. |
| **Achilles' Veil** | 2025 | Farid | Maintain deep-cover status inside Cordis Die in Yemen. |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Steps to Achieve the Best Ending">

To maximize your campaign run and secure the most favorable ending, you must make a specific sequence of decisions across several key missions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Interrogate Kravchenko">
    In the 1986 mission "Old Wounds," resist the brainwashing numbers conditioning. Tap the prompt repeatedly to avoid executing Lev Kravchenko prematurely, allowing you to discover the CIA mole.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Save Chloe Lynch (Karma)">
    In the 2025 mission "Karma," eliminate DeFalco before he escapes with Chloe. If you fail, you must immediately play and succeed in the "Second Chance" Strike Force mission to rescue her.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spare Alex Mason">
    In the 1989 mission "Suffer with Me," as Frank Woods, aim your sniper rifle at the hooded target's legs. Shoot twice in the legs to ensure Alex Mason survives the encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Sacrifice Harper as Farid">
    In the 2025 mission "Achilles' Veil," as Farid, choose to shoot Harper. This sacrifice keeps Farid's cover intact, allowing him to protect Chloe Lynch later on the USS Barack Obama.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Wound Admiral Briggs">
    In the 2025 mission "Odysseus," as Raul Menendez, shoot Admiral Briggs in the leg rather than executing him. This keeps the carrier defenses operational and preserves the ship.
  </Step>
</Steps>
