<Section icon={Target} title="Strike Force Operations and Geopolitical Alliances">

Strike Force missions are tactical, objective-based scenarios that open up dynamically as you progress through the main campaign. Failing to complete these before executing main missions can cause them to expire, which negatively impacts the final political landscape of the 2025 Cold War.

| Strike Force Mission | Setting | Unit Assets | Strategic Story Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **FOB Spectre** | India | Infantry, CLAWs, Turrets | Defends the base to weaken SDC military aggression in the region. |
| **Shipwreck** | Singapore | Ground & Aerial Drones | Secures the cargo docks to reduce SDC naval pressure. |
| **I.E.D.** | Afghanistan | Escort Convoy, Drones | Protects Allied assets to secure a US-China defense alliance. |
| **Second Chance** | Yemen | Rescue Infantry | Only appears if Chloe Lynch is captured; vital for keeping Karma alive. |
| **Dispatch** | Pakistan | JSOC Strike Squad | Eliminates Chairman Tian Zhao to secure cooperation with Chinese forces. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Strike Force Expiration">
Strike Force missions must be completed before starting Mission 9 (Odysseus). Once you begin the assault on the USS Barack Obama, any uncompleted Strike Force missions will fail automatically, locking you out of the best ending.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Critical Campaign Decisions Step-by-Step">

To achieve the best possible outcome in your Call of Duty Black Ops II walkthrough, you must make specific choices during key missions. Use the step-by-step path below to ensure the survival of key characters and secure a peaceful resolution to the Second Cold War.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Old Wounds: Resist the Numbers">
    During the interrogation of Lev Kravchenko in Afghanistan, rapidly tap the prompt to resist the Numbers conditioning. This prevents Mason from shooting him prematurely, allowing you to discover that Menendez has a mole inside the CIA.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Karma: Secure Chloe Lynch">
    Pursue DeFalco quickly through the Colossus resort. If you fail to kill DeFalco before he escapes with Chloe (Karma), you must immediately complete the optional **Second Chance** Strike Force mission to rescue her.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Suffer with Me: Shoot the Legs">
    When playing as Frank Woods in Panama, you will be ordered to take a sniper shot at a hooded target. **Do not shoot him in the head.** Aim for the target's legs twice. This allows Alex Mason to survive the encounter and return in the 2025 epilogue.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Achilles' Veil: Sacrifice Harper">
    When playing undercover as Farid, Menendez will order you to execute Harper. **Shoot Harper instead of attempting to shoot Menendez.** This sacrifice keeps Farid alive, which is required to shield Chloe Lynch from Salazar's betrayal later on the USS Barack Obama.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Odysseus: Spare Admiral Briggs">
    When taking control of Raul Menendez on the carrier, aim for Admiral Briggs' leg instead of executing him. Sparing Briggs allows him to reactivate the carrier's automated defenses, ensuring the survival of the USS Barack Obama.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Judgment Day: Capture Menendez">
    At the climax of the final assault in Haiti, you will corner Raul Menendez. **Choose to capture Menendez rather than executing him.** If you execute him, his pre-recorded video will trigger a devastating worldwide Cordis Die uprising.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="The Favorable Path">
If you successfully execute all six steps, Alex Mason survives, Chloe Lynch defeats Menendez's cyberattack, the USS Barack Obama is saved, and the United States and China form a lasting alliance after Chairman Zhao is eliminated.
</Callout>

</Section>
