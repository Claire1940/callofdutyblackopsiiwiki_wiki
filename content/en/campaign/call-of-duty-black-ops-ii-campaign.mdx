<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Unlock the Best Ending">

Unlocking the optimal "Favorable Ending" in the **call of duty black ops ii campaign** requires a perfect run across multiple missions. Use this step-by-step sequence to ensure all key characters survive and the global cyber threat is neutralized.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the US-China Alliance">
    Complete the Strike Force missions: FOB Spectre, Shipwreck, and I.E.D. This unlocks the "Dispatch" mission. Complete "Dispatch" to eliminate Chairman Tian Zhao, bringing China into an alliance with the US.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Save Chloe Lynch (Karma)">
    Either eliminate DeFalco during the "Karma" mission before he escapes with Chloe, or successfully complete the "Second Chance" Strike Force rescue mission to secure her.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spare Alex Mason">
    During "Suffer with Me," when instructed to take the sniper shot on the hooded target, shoot the target in the legs twice. Do not shoot the head.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Keep Farid Alive">
    In "Achilles' Veil," make the difficult choice to shoot Harper. This sacrifice keeps Farid's cover intact, allowing him to sacrifice himself to save Chloe on the USS Barack Obama later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Defend the USS Barack Obama">
    In "Odysseus," shoot Admiral Briggs in the leg. This keeps him alive. Because the US-China alliance is active and Chloe is protected by Farid, the carrier survives Salazar's betrayal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Capture Raul Menendez">
    In the final mission "Judgment Day," choose to capture Menendez instead of executing him. Chloe will successfully neutralize his cyberattack, and Menendez will remain securely imprisoned.
  </Step>
</Steps>
