<Section icon={User} title="Mike Harper: Background and Character Profile">

Master Chief Petty Officer **Mike Harper** serves as one of the most charismatic and loyal protagonists in the 2025 narrative arc of the game. Born in 1987 in Arkansas, his early life was shaped by hardship and the mentorship of his uncle Finn, a Vietnam War veteran. This background forged Harper into a resilient soldier with a penchant for boxing and a deep-seated loyalty to his squad mates, particularly David "Section" Mason.

In the context of **call of duty black ops ii harper** represents the "boots on the ground" perspective of the future war. He is often the one providing tactical advice, comic relief, or raw aggression when the mission parameters shift. His relationship with Section is built on years of shared deployments, making him more than just a subordinate; he is Section's primary confidant and tactical anchor.

<Section icon={Map} title="Key Missions and Story Progression">

Throughout the campaign, Harper is present for the most critical operations aimed at dismantling Raul Menendez's "Cordis Die" organization. His involvement spans from the initial investigation of Frank Woods to the final assault on Menendez's facility in Haiti.

**Major Campaign Appearances:**
- **Celerium**: Harper assists Section in recovering the quantum entanglement device in Myanmar, showcasing his proficiency with future tech like the wingsuit and optical camo.
- **Anthem**: During the pursuit of Menendez in Pakistan, Harper suffers significant burns to the right side of his face due to a burst pipe, a moment that defines his visual appearance for the rest of the game.
- **Colossus**: He plays a vital role in identifying Chloe "Karma" Lynch, even attempting to charm her before the mission turns into a high-stakes shootout against DeFalco's mercenaries.

<Section icon={Target} title="The Achilles' Veil Choice: Sparing vs. Killing Harper">

The most significant moment involving **call of duty black ops ii harper** occurs during the mission "Achilles' Veil." As the player controls the undercover operative Farid, Raul Menendez discovers a potential traitor and orders Farid to execute a captured Harper. This choice is a cornerstone of the game's branching narrative system.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Confrontation">
    Menendez brings Farid to a captured Harper. He hands Farid a weapon and demands he prove his loyalty by killing his JSOC comrade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Decision Point">
    The player must choose to either "Kill Harper" or "Attempt to Shoot Menendez."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execution of Choice">
    If you kill Harper, Farid remains undercover. If you attempt to shoot Menendez, Menendez executes Farid, and Harper is eventually rescued.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Harper's Role in the Defense of the USS Obama">

If Harper survives the incident in Yemen, he plays a crucial role during the mission "Suffer with Me" and the subsequent defense of the USS Barack Obama. His presence significantly alters the dialogue and the emotional weight of the betrayal subplot involving Javier Salazar.

When Salazar reveals himself as a mole for Cordis Die and surrenders, a surviving Harper does not hesitate. In a moment of pure retribution, Harper executes Salazar on the spot, citing the lives lost due to his treachery. This scene is one of the most memorable character moments for Harper, reinforcing his "no-nonsense" approach to warfare and loyalty.

| Event | Harper Alive | Harper Deceased |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Ship Defense** | Harper provides active fire support | Standard JSOC NPCs provide support |
| **Dialogue** | Unique interactions with Section | Section reflects on Harper's loss |
| **Final Mission** | Harper joins the Haiti assault | Harper is replaced by generic lead |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beyond Black Ops II: Appearances in Black Ops 7">

The story of **call of duty black ops ii harper** doesn't end in 2025. According to expanded lore and the events of *Black Ops 7*, Harper transitioned into a mentorship role within JSOC before returning to active duty. By 2035, he is a member of "Specter One," still serving alongside David Mason.

In this later era, Harper has undergone cybernetic enhancements, including a C-Link implant to maintain his combat edge despite his age. He remains a playable Operator in Multiplayer and *Warzone 2.0*, often featuring skins that reference his classic *Black Ops II* appearance.

**Notable Black Ops 7 Skins:**
- **Blowback**: A legendary skin based on his original 2025 JSOC outfit.
- **Old Man**: A skin representing his appearance as a veteran mentor in 2035.
- **Survivor**: A glitched skin that occasionally appears, showing his battle-worn campaign gear.
