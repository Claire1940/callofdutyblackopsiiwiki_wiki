<Section icon={Map} title="Call of Duty Black Ops II Mission List Overview">

The campaign features a unique structure that blends traditional linear storytelling with a branching narrative system. To complete the story, players navigate through 11 main missions spanning two distinct eras: the 1980s Cold War following Alex Mason, and a near-future 2025 Second Cold War centered on his son, David Mason. 

Alongside the main storyline, the campaign introduces five optional tactical missions known as Strike Force operations. These tasks require players to command various JSOC assets to secure key strategic zones.

| Mission Name | Era | Playable Character | Story Significance |

| **Pyrrhic Victory** | 1986 | Alex Mason | Introduces Raul Menendez and rescues Frank Woods |

| **Old Wounds** | 1986 | Alex Mason | Interrogates Kravchenko/reveals CIA mole |
| **Time and Fate** | 1986 | Raul Menendez / Alex Mason | Details the origins of Menendez's global hatred |
| **Fallen Angel** | 2025 | David Mason | Unlocks tactical paths through surveillance |
| **Karma** | 2025 | David Mason | Determines the capture or safety of Chloe Lynch |

<Section icon={Target} title="Strike Force Missions and Tactical Impact">

Strike Force operations are real-time tactical missions where you command infantry squads, automated defense turrets, CLAW units, and aerial drones. Successfully completing these operations weakens the Strategic Defense Coalition (SDC) presence and secures crucial support for the final stages of the game.

These missions must be completed before starting the main mission **Odysseus**, as they expire once the story progresses past that point.

| Strike Force Mission | Location | Key Objective | Story Influence |
|---|---|---|---|
| **FOB Spectre** | India | Defend the forward operating base from SDC forces | Protects regional JSOC command centers |
| **Shipwreck** | Singapore | Capture and hold the marked cargo dock areas | Disrupts SDC shipping routes and naval power |
| **I.E.D.** | Afghanistan | Escort and protect the military convoy | Weakens SDC regional influence and secures alliances |
| **Second Chance** | Yemen | Rescue Chloe Lynch (if captured during Karma) | Restores the branch where Karma remains alive |
| **Dispatch** | Pakistan | Infiltrate command site and eliminate Tian Zhao | Allows China and the United States to unite |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
If you fail or skip these Strike Force missions before they expire, the geopolitical landscape in the ending epilogue will shift unfavorably, making a US-China alliance impossible.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Key Campaign Decisions and Ending Requirements">

To secure the most favorable outcome in the campaign, you must satisfy a chain of specific requirements across multiple missions. These choices directly affect character survival, military readiness, and the global political state.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Alex Mason Fate">
    In **Suffer with Me**, shoot the hooded target in the legs twice instead of delivering a headshot. This allows Alex Mason to survive and return in the 2025 epilogue.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Karma Protection">
    Save Chloe Lynch during **Karma** or complete **Second Chance**. Additionally, Farid must shoot Harper in **Achilles' Veil** to protect her later on the USS Barack Obama.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Raul Menendez Choice">
    At the conclusion of **Judgment Day**, capture Menendez instead of executing him. If Karma is alive, she will successfully stop his cyberattack from prison.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Navigate Branching Campaign Outcomes">

If you want to change a specific decision to view alternative endings, you can utilize the Replay Story feature. Follow these steps to adjust your campaign trajectory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Mission Select Screen">
    Go to the Campaign menu and open the mission selection dashboard to view your completed operations and current story state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rewind to the Target Mission">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Alternative Choice">
    Play through the mission and select the new path (e.g., shooting the target in the legs during Suffer with Me or sparing Admiral Briggs in Odysseus).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Remaining Missions">
    Play through the rest of the campaign sequentially to carry the new decisions forward into the final epilogue sequences.
  </Step>
</Steps>
