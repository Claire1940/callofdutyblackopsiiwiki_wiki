<Section icon={Target} title="TranZit and Die Rise Main Quest Steps">

TranZit serves as the introduction to the Victis crew and the "Tower of Babble" quest. This map is unique due to its massive size and the reliance on the bus for transportation. To begin the Easter Egg, players must first turn on the power at the Power Station. From there, the path splits between Dr. Maxis and Edward Richtofen. Maxis requires a more tactical approach with Turbines, while Richtofen demands the use of the Jet Gun and explosives.

Die Rise takes the fight to a vertical level in a ruined Chinese city. The "High Maintenance" quest involves manipulating elevators and rooftop symbols. This map is notorious for its fall hazards, making the **Trample Steam** buildable an essential tool for both survival and quest progression.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Survival Strategies for Mob of the Dead and Buried">

Mob of the Dead, set in Alcatraz, is widely considered one of the best maps in the series. Its Easter Egg, "Pop Goes the Weasel," follows the four mobsters as they attempt to break a supernatural cycle. The core mechanic here is the **Afterlife** mode, which allows players to power up machines and find hidden parts while in a downed state.

Buried takes players to an underground Western town. The "Mined Games" quest heavily involves Arthur, the friendly giant. By feeding him candy or booze, players can manipulate him to break barriers or assist in buildable construction. This map provides a more relaxed pace due to the ability to "bank" points and store weapons in a locker for future matches.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Origins: The Little Lost Girl Quest Guide">

Origins is the most complex map in the Black Ops II Zombies saga, requiring players to build and upgrade four Elemental Staffs. The "Little Lost Girl" quest is a multi-stage process that culminates in a massive battle inside the "Crazy Place." Successfully completing this quest provides the definitive ending to the Black Ops II Zombies storyline.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Staffs">
    Collect the three parts and the corresponding record for the Ice, Fire, Wind, and Lightning Staffs. Build them at the bottom of the Excavation Site.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Ultimate Upgrade">
    Solve the puzzles in the Crazy Place and the main map to upgrade all four staffs into their "Ultimate" forms, such as Ull's Arrow or Boreas' Fury.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="G-Strike and Rain Fire">
    Obtain the G-Strike beacons by cleaning the stone tablets. Coordinate with your team to throw a beacon at the seal in the robot's foot while someone else presses the button inside the robot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Purge">
    Release the Maxis Drone into the portal in the Crazy Place and eliminate 100 zombies within the central area to charge the final portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>
