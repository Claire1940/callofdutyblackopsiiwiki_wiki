<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Pack-a-Punch Activation Guide">

Unlocking the Pack-a-Punch machine is essential for surviving high rounds, as it upgrades your weapons for 5,000 points. The activation process varies heavily across the different environments of **call of duty black ops ii zombies**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="TranZit Bank Vault Route">
    Open the Town bank vault doors using explosives, place a Turbine at the green lightning door in the Power Station, and enter the newly opened underground laboratory below the bank to assemble the machine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mob of the Dead Plane Assembly">
    Enter Afterlife mode to power key doors, collect the five scattered plane parts across Alcatraz, assemble the Icarus plane on the prison roof, and fly to the Golden Gate Bridge where the machine sits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Buried Mansion Navigation">
    Earn enough points to clear the debris, travel through the haunted mansion while avoiding the ghost's point-draining attacks, cross the hedge maze, and descend into the underground gazebo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Origins Generator Conquest">
    Locate and activate all six steam generators scattered across the muddy trenches. Defend them from attacking Templar Zombies to keep the Pack-a-Punch machine operational at the central Excavation Site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Power Play">
Upgraded weapons not only deal significantly more damage but often gain completely new firing profiles, increased magazine capacities, and special attachments.
</Callout>

</Section>
