<Section icon={Zap} title="Activating Power and Pack-a-Punch">

Activating the main power is a prerequisite for most endgame activities in Call of Duty Black Ops II Zombies TranZit, but the Pack-a-Punch machine requires an additional, time-sensitive step that often frustrates new players. Following a specific sequence is necessary to ensure the vault door in the Town remains open.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Power Switch">
    Travel to the Power Station (the fourth stop). Drop into the laboratory and find the three parts: a severed zombie arm, a switch lever, and a circuit board. Assemble them at the workbench nearby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Turbine Placement">
    Before turning on the power, one player must stay at the Power Station. Another player must go to the Town. The player at the Power Station drops a Turbine in front of the green lightning door inside the laboratory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access the Vault">
    While the Turbine is active at the Power Station, the vault door in the Town's Bank (behind the counter) will lose its power lock. Use a grenade to blow open the two initial bank vault doors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assemble the Machine">
    Inside the underground lab beneath the bank, collect the three Pack-a-Punch parts: the battery, the wooden base, and the machine head. Assemble them at the workbench at the back of the room.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pack-a-Punch Persistence">
Once the Pack-a-Punch machine is built, it stays built for the rest of the match. However, if the Turbine at the Power Station is destroyed, the vault door will close, and you will need to place a new Turbine to re-enter.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Bus} title="Mastering the Bus and Fog Navigation">

The automated bus is your primary lifeline in Call of Duty Black Ops II Zombies TranZit, but it can also be a death trap if you are caught unprepared. Understanding the bus upgrades and the dangers of the fog is essential for high-round survival.

**Bus Upgrade Parts:**
- **Ladder**: Allows players to climb to the roof of the bus from the outside. Found in the "parts rooms" that require a Turbine to open.
- **Cattle Catcher**: Prevents zombies from jumping through the front windshield.
- **Hatch**: Allows access to the roof from the inside. *Note: If used on the bus, it cannot be used at the Diner to access the Galvaknuckles.*

| Hazard | Description | Counter-Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Denizens** | Small creatures that latch onto your head in the fog | Melee 5 times or use Galvaknuckles for a 2-hit kill |
| **The Avogadro** | An electric zombie that appears when power is on | Use EMP grenades or melee him while he is solid |
| **Lava Cracks** | Ground hazards that deal fire damage | Jump over them or use the Stamin-Up perk |
| **Bus Leaving** | The bus honks twice before departing | Ensure all teammates are aboard or have a teleport ready |

<Callout type="info" title="Teleportation Trick">
You can teleport between locations by jumping under a green streetlamp while a Denizen is on your head. This creates a portal that takes you to a random lamp elsewhere on the map.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Survival and Banking Tips">

For players looking to reach round 30 and beyond in Call of Duty Black Ops II Zombies TranZit, mastering the persistent systems like the Bank and the Weapon Locker is non-negotiable. These features allow you to carry over resources from previous games, which is a unique mechanic of the Victis crew maps.

**The Banking System:**
- Located in the Town's Bank.
- **Deposit**: Stand near the two teller windows and hold the interact button to deposit 1,000 points (costs a 100-point fee).
- **Withdrawal**: Go to the side of the vault and interact with the "keys" on the floor to take out points.
- **Sharing**: You can use the Galvaknuckles on the two "punch" icons in the back of the bank to create a point drop for teammates.

**The Weapon Locker:**
- Located in the Farm house (inside the fridge).
- You can store one weapon here. It will remain in the fridge across different matches until you retrieve it.
- *Pro Tip*: Store a Pack-a-Punched weapon like the Mustang and Sally or the DSR 50 to give yourself a massive advantage in early rounds of a new game.
