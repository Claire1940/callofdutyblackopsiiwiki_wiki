<Section icon={Map} title="Origins Map Overview and Core Mechanics">

The definitive final chapter of the Zombies saga in **call of duty black ops ii** takes players to Northern France in 1918. In **call of duty black ops ii origins**, you control the younger Primis versions of Dempsey, Nikolai, Takeo, and Richtofen as they navigate a muddy, trench-filled battlefield devastated by Element 115. 

Unlike traditional maps, Origins does not feature a master power switch. Instead, players must activate six different 115 Generators scattered across the dig site to power local perks, mystery boxes, and the Pack-a-Punch machine.

| Generator | Location | Adjacent Perks & Utilities |
|-|-|-|
| **Generator 1** | Spawn Room | Quick Revive, Shovel spawns |
| **Generator 2** | Tank Station | Stamin-Up, Mystery Box location |
| **Generator 3** | Fire Tunnel Trenches | Speed Cola, Shield part spawn |
| **Generator 4** | Wind Tunnel | Juggernog, Der Wunderfizz |
| **Generator 5** | Lightning Tunnel | Stamin-Up, Wunderfizz, Stamina trials |
| **Generator 6** | Church Yard | Double Tap, Mark IV Tank stop |

<Callout type="warning" title="Generator Defense">
Once all six generators are active, Templar Zombies will periodically spawn to attack and deactivate them. Defend your generators to keep the Pack-a-Punch machine active at the top of the Excavation Mound.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Origins Setup Guide">

Getting set up efficiently in the early rounds is critical to surviving the mid-to-late game challenges of **call of duty black ops ii origins**. Follow this optimal pathing sequence to maximize your resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Spawn Area">
    Grab the shovels in the spawn room, activate Generator 1, and farm points using the Mauser C96 and melee attacks. Do not open the doors until you have at least 2,500 points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire the Zombie Shield">
    Open the path toward Generator 2 or 3. Search the trenches and the Workshop to find the three shield parts. Build the Zombie Shield at the workbench inside the Workshop to protect your back.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Power Up Juggernog">
    Head to Generator 4 in No Man's Land. Activate the generator to turn on the Juggernog machine. Purchase Juggernog immediately for 2,500 points to increase your health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare for the Panzer Soldat">
    On Round 8, the first Panzer Soldat will drop. Ensure you have a high-damage weapon like the MP40, STG-44, or an early Elemental Staff to destroy his helmet and core before he grabs you with his claw.
  </Step>
</Steps>
