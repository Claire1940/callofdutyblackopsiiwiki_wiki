<Section icon={Map} title="Introduction to Kino der Toten">

Originally released in *Call of Duty: Black Ops* and remastered for *Call of Duty: Black Ops III* in the Zombies Chronicles pack, Kino der Toten (German for Cinema of the Dead) remains one of the most iconic cooperative survival maps in gaming history. Set in an abandoned Group 935 theater facility in 1963 Germany, the map challenges players to fight off relentless undead hordes. 

Whether you are playing the classic version or the remastered edition, understanding the layout and flow of the map is critical. The map introduces unique elements like Crawler Zombies, which release toxic gas upon death, and the Fire Pit trap.

| Edition | Key Weapons | Unique Features | Enemy Types |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Black Ops** | M1911, MP40, Thundergun | Classic Cold War Wall Buys | Classic Zombies, Crawlers, Hellhounds |
| **Black Ops III** | 1911, RK5, Kuda, Thundergun | GobbleGums, Wunderfizz, Remastered AI | Aggressive Zombies, Crawlers, Hellhounds |

<Callout type="tip" title="Room Control">

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Power and Pack-a-Punch Guide">

Opening the Pack-a-Punch machine is your main objective during the early rounds. Follow these steps to turn on the power, link the teleporter, and upgrade your weapons.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accumulate Points in Spawn">
    Stay in the Lobby until Round 4 or 5. Use your pistol to shoot zombies in the leg 5-6 times before finishing them with a knife to maximize points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Path to the Theater">
    Open the upper doors through the Upper Hall and Dressing Room, or take the lower route through the Library. Make your way directly to the Stage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Turn on the Power">
    Locate the power switch on the metal wall near the Stage curtain. Activating it opens the curtain, turns on traps, and activates the teleporter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Link the Teleporter">
    Step inside the Teleporter on the Stage and initiate the link. Run back to the Lobby (Spawn) and interact with the launch pad on the floor to complete the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Pack-a-Punch and Escape">
    Use the linked teleporter to travel to the Projector Room. Spend 5000 points to upgrade your weapon. You will have approximately 30 seconds before being teleported back out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Random Room Reels">
When teleporting back from the Projector Room, you may land in a random room (like the Dentist Room or Conference Room). Search these rooms quickly for collectible film reels to insert into the projector.
</Callout>

</Section>
