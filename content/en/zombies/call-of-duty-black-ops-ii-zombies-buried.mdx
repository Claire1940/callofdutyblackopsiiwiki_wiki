<Section icon={Map} title="Buried Map Overview and Setting">

**Call of duty black ops ii zombies buried** is the third major DLC map for the Zombies mode, included in the Vengeance map pack. The setting is a subterranean Old West mining town located in Africa, featuring iconic locations like a Saloon, a Courthouse, a General Store, and a sprawling Haunted Mansion. Unlike the verticality of Die Rise, Buried focuses on tight corridors, underground tunnels, and a complex hedge maze leading to the Pack-a-Punch machine.

The map introduced several revolutionary mechanics to the series, including the ability to manipulate a friendly NPC and a permanent bank system that allows players to store and retrieve points across different matches. Whether you are playing the original PC version or the 2026 PlayStation port, understanding the layout of the town is essential for survival.

| Location | Key Features | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quick Revive Area** | Initial spawn, basic weapons | Safe for early rounds only |
| **General Store** | All buildable parts are stored here | Essential for crafting equipment |
| **Saloon** | Booze spawn, Arthur's secondary food | Central hub for town navigation |
| **Haunted Mansion** | Ghost encounters, point drain | Mandatory path to Pack-a-Punch |
| **Hedge Maze** | Changing paths, fountain portal | Final barrier before weapon upgrades |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Use the fountain located in front of the Mansion and the one in the Maze to create a teleportation link, allowing you to bypass the ghosts when returning from the Pack-a-Punch area.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Wonder Weapons and Best Loadouts">

In **call of duty black ops ii zombies buried**, the arsenal is divided between classic wall weapons and high-tech wonder weapons. The Paralyzer is the standout choice, as it does not use traditional ammunition and provides the player with vertical mobility.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Paralyzer">
    Acquire this from the Mystery Box. It slows down zombies and eventually disintegrates them. Use it to fly by aiming at the ground and jumping.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip the Ray Gun Mark II">
    Available if you own the Vengeance DLC. This burst-fire weapon is highly effective against the Panzer-like toughness of high-round zombies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build the Subsurface Resonator">
    Combine the parts found in the General Store. When powered by a Turbine, it emits sonic blasts that clear entire hallways.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize the Time Bomb">
    This tactical equipment allows you to "save" a game state. If the team goes down, you can detonate it to revert back to the moment you placed it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="High Round Strategy">
The combination of the Paralyzer and the Subsurface Resonator is the meta for reaching round 100+. Place the Resonator in the hallway behind the Saloon for a nearly impenetrable defense.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Mined Games Easter Egg Steps">

The main quest in Buried, titled "Mined Games," continues the conflict between Dr. Richtofen and Maxis. It requires a four-player team and involves complex coordination within the town and the maze.

| Step | Requirement | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Power Up** | Turbine / Power Switch | Activate the map's main power and build the gallows |
| **2. The Orbs** | Paralyzer / Ray Gun | Charge the four hidden orbs located around the town |
| **3. The Lantern** | Grenades / Melee | Capture the floating lantern and charge it with souls |
| **4. The Maze** | Coordination | Navigate the hedge maze and flick switches in order |
| **5. The Sharpshooter** | Accuracy | Simultaneously shoot all targets during the western standoff |

<Callout type="warning" title="Easter Egg Warning">
The final "Sharpshooter" step is notoriously difficult. If a single player misses a target, the step resets. Use the Time Bomb to reset to the start of the step if you fail.
</Callout>
