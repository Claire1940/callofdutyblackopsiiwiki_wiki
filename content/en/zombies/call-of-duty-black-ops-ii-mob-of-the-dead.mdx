<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering the Afterlife Mechanic">

The defining feature of **call of duty black ops ii mob of the dead** is the Afterlife mode. When a player "dies" or uses an electrical box, they enter a spectral state. In this form, you can levitate, pass through certain walls, and blast electrical energy to power machines or interact with the environment. Managing your Afterlife charges is critical for early-game setup.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Powering Perks">
    Perk machines in Alcatraz do not start powered. You must enter Afterlife near the machine and shock the power box to activate it permanently for that game.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Opening Portals">
    Look for blue shimmering walls or doors. In Afterlife, you can walk through these to reach hidden areas containing plane parts or power switches.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Speeding Up Revives">
    In Afterlife, you can revive yourself by interacting with your own body. If playing co-op, you can also shock teammates' bodies to revive them instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Environmental Puzzles">
    Certain doors, like the Warden's Office or the Laundry Room, require specific electrical sequences only visible or interactable while in spectral form.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

| Interaction | Location | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shock Box** | Near Juggernog | Powers the Juggernog machine |
| **Portal** | Library | Access to the Warden's Key |
| **Power Switch** | Roof | Activates the elevator/plane ramp |
| **Voltage Meter** | Warden's Office | Opens the door to the office |

<Callout type="warning" title="Charge Limits">
In solo play, you receive three Afterlife charges per round. In multiplayer, you only get one. Use them wisely, especially when trying to build the plane early.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Building the Icarus Plane">

To upgrade your weapons in **call of duty black ops ii mob of the dead**, you must fly to the Golden Gate Bridge. This requires collecting five parts scattered across the prison and assembling them on the roof.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Warden's Key">
    The key hangs outside the Warden's Office or near the Cafeteria. Enter Afterlife, jump through the portal above the key, and shock the power box to lower it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect the Five Parts">
    Use the Warden's Key to open specialized lockers and cages containing the Uniform, Engine, Oxygen Tank, Valves, and Rigging.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the Roof">
    Head to the Infirmary and unlock the door to the roof. You will need to enter Afterlife one last time to power the ramp switch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assemble and Launch">
    Place all five parts on the wooden frame. All players must stand on the plane to initiate the flight to the bridge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Part Name | Location | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Uniform** | Laundry Room | Start the washing machine cycle and survive |
| **Engine** | Warden's Office | Unlock the electrical door in Afterlife |
| **Oxygen Tank** | Docks | Two players or fast Afterlife movement needed |
| **Valves** | Citadel Tunnels | Enter the code 115, 935, or 872 in the dial |
| **Rigging** | Spiral Stairs | Use the Warden's Key on the hanging cage |

<Callout type="success" title="Refueling Tip">
Once you return from the bridge, the plane parts will reappear as canisters around the map. You must collect them again to make subsequent trips to Pack-a-Punch.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="High Round Survival Strategies">

Surviving past round 50 in **call of duty black ops ii mob of the dead** requires a solid "training" route or a camping spot with high-damage traps. The most popular strategy involves the Cafeteria or the Golden Gate Bridge.

**The Cafeteria Strategy:**
This is the most reliable solo route. By keeping the door to the infirmary closed, you can loop zombies around the tables. Use the Acidgat to fire at the floor, drawing zombies away from you, and then use the Acid Trap to clear the horde.

**The Bridge Strategy:**
The Bridge is the only area where no boss (Brutus) spawns during the rounds. However, there are no traps or wall-buys. Players usually stay here to upgrade the Hell's Retriever or to burn through ammo before returning to the prison via the Electric Chairs.

| Strategy Type | Location | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Training** | Cafeteria | Easy access to Acid Trap | Tight corners can be lethal |
| **Camping** | Gondola | Controlled chokepoint | Limited escape if overrun |
| **Training** | Docks | Large open space | Brutus spawns frequently here |

<Callout type="warning" title="Brutus the Warden">
Brutus is a heavy boss who spawns every few rounds or when you spend too much at the Mystery Box. He can "lock" perk machines and crafting tables, requiring 2000 points to reopen them. Aim for his helmet to expose his head for a quick kill.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Pop Goes the Weasel Easter Egg">

The main quest of **call of duty black ops ii mob of the dead** is titled "Pop Goes the Weasel." It is unique because it is the first Zombies Easter Egg that can actually be "completed" with a definitive ending.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Prerequisites">
    Build the plane and fly to the bridge at least three times. All players must have the Hell's Retriever and the Blundergat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Numbers">
    In the Citadel Tunnels, use the dial to enter the prisoner numbers of the four characters (found on their cells).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Audio Logs">
    Collect the white orbs (headphones) around the map to listen to the story of the failed escape attempt.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Flight">
    All players enter Afterlife at the same time and board the plane. This takes you to the Bridge for the final battle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Break the Cycle">
    On the bridge, the three mobsters must fight the Weasel. If the Weasel wins, the cycle is broken. If the mobsters win, the cycle continues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
