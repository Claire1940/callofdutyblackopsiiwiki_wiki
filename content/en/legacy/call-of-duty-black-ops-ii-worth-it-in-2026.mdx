<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Getting Started: 2026 New Player Checklist">

If you are picking up Call of Duty Black Ops II for the first time on PS5 or returning after a decade, follow these steps to optimize your experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Adjust Your Settings">
    Configure your horizontal and vertical sensitivity. Start at **4 or 5** and increase as you get comfortable. Turn off "Vibration" for more precise aiming in Multiplayer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Create-a-Class">
    Play public matches until you reach **Level 4**. This unlocks the Pick 10 system, allowing you to move away from default classes and build specialized loadouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Scorestreaks">
    Begin with low-cost streaks like the **UAV, Hunter Killer Drone, and Counter-UAV**. These provide consistent utility and are easier to earn as you learn the map layouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Learn the Maps">
    Focus on learning the "three-lane" layouts of maps like **Raid and Standoff**. Controlling the center lane while monitoring the flanking routes is the key to high-kill games.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Professional Advice">
Watch the "Killcam" when you die. It is the fastest way to learn popular camping spots, effective head-glitches, and how higher-level players move around the map.
</Callout>
