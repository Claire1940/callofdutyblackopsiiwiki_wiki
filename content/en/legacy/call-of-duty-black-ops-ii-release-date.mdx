<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Roadmap to Combat Readiness">

Whether you are a returning veteran or a recruit stepping onto the battlefield for the first time, starting your journey efficiently will keep you ahead of the competition. Follow these structured steps to configure your settings, master the modes, and level up quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Optimize Controls and Sensitivity">
    Head to the options menu before entering a match. Set a comfortable look sensitivity (start around 4 or 5) and adjust your display brightness so that dark corners in maps like Plaza and Express remain visible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Pick 10 System">
    Once you reach Level 4 in Multiplayer, Create-a-Class unlocks. You have exactly 10 allocation points to spend on primary weapons, attachments, perks, and equipment. Keep your loadout simple at first by prioritizing a strong primary weapon and three basic perks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Learn Zombies Point Economy">
    In round-based Zombies, points are your lifeline. Earn points by landing shots and securing melee kills on early rounds. Prioritize buying a reliable wall weapon like the MP5 or M14, and secure the Jugger-Nog perk as soon as you open up the map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Recommended Starter Class">
For a balanced start in Multiplayer, pair the MTAR assault rifle with a Reflex Sight, Quickdraw Handle, and Fast Mag. Use the Flak Jacket, Toughness, and Dexterity perks to survive explosive spam and win head-on gunfights.
