<Section icon={Map} title="Mission 1 to 4: Early Campaign Intel">

The first few missions set the stage for the dual-timeline narrative. You will switch between Alex Mason in the 1980s and David Mason in 2025. Each era has distinct environmental hazards that can obscure **call of duty black ops 2 intel locations**.

| Mission | Intel 1 Location | Intel 2 Location | Intel 3 Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pyrrhic Victory** | Near the mortar truck | Gunboat upper deck | Sniper perch in forest |
| **Celerium** | Helipad side room | Underground office desk | Exit of Celerium chamber |
| **Old Wounds** | Cave briefing crate | Red flag in desert | Right-side cliff cave |
| **Time and Fate** | Rear of barn wagon | Bell tower second level | Cocaine lab side bench |

**Detailed Breakdown:**

- **Pyrrhic Victory**: The first item is revealed by a mortar blast near the truck where you get shells. The second is on the gunboat; climb the ladder and look opposite the gun emplacement. The third is during the forest retreat; check the second sniper perch on the right side.
- **Celerium**: After the helipad fight, check the small room to the left. Deep in the lab, look for a room with a large staircase; the intel is in an office to the left. The final one is at the far end of the facility after the Celerium reveal.
- **Old Wounds**: Grab the first one from a crate immediately after the opening cutscene. The second is tricky; ride into the desert and look for a red flag among rocks. The third is in a cave on the right side of the cliffside bridges.
- **Time and Fate**: As Raul, find the first laptop on a wagon at the back of the barn. As Mason, climb the bell tower in the courtyard for the second. The third is in the basement cocaine facility, in a small room on the far left.

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Surroundings">
In "Old Wounds," the desert intel is very easy to miss due to the sand and scale of the map. Use the red flag as your primary landmark; it stands out against the beige rocks.
</Callout>

</Section>

**Detailed Breakdown:**

- **Fallen Angel**: The first is on a shelf right where you start with Maximus. The second is in a small opening on the right after dodging the floating bus. The third is on the ground at the end of the flooded street, just before entering the wall opening.
- **Karma**: Find the first laptop in the server room on a desk before the elevator. The second is inside the "d'HO" store on the lower mall level. The third is located in the computer room where you fight through to find Karma; check the desks on the right side.
- **Suffer With Me**: After the skylight entrance, check the office shelf at the end of the catwalk. The second is in a building with a hole in the wall near the church courtyard. The third is on a table right after the clinic cutscene.
- **Achilles Veil**: The first is on the ground by a staircase in the large courtyard. The second is tucked in a corner of a room with a TV screen behind a soldier. The third is on a roof; take the path leading down, then climb the ladder behind the building.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mission 9 to 11: Final Campaign Intel">

The concluding chapters of the game feature high-tech environments like the U.S.S. Barack Obama and the futuristic city of Los Angeles. These **call of duty black ops 2 intel locations** require careful movement through corridors and plazas.

| Mission | Intel 1 Location | Intel 2 Location | Intel 3 Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Odysseus** | Bridge console | Room above hanger | VTOL rear ramp |
| **Cordis Die** | Rappel level car | Lobby counter | Plaza shirt vendor |
| **Judgement Day** | Building 27 floor 2 | Broadcast row 3 | Circular room crate |

**Detailed Breakdown:**

- **Odysseus**: In the Comm Room, clear the enemies and head to the bridge; the intel is on the right console. The second is in a small room reached by a staircase just before heading down to the hanger. The third is inside the second VTOL on the deck lift, right near the cockpit door.
- **Cordis Die**: After rappelling, look behind the burned-out car on the left. The second is on the central counter of the first lobby you enter. The third is on the second floor of the Downtown Plaza, sitting on a shirt vendor's stall.
- **Judgement Day**: Enter Building 27 and head to the second floor to find the first laptop. In the broadcast room, check the third row of consoles from the top. The final intel in the game is in the circular room after the broadcast area; look for a crate glowing in the dark near the stairs.

<Callout type="tip" title="Final Push">
In "Judgement Day," the broadcast room is a massive firelight. Don't rush for the intel until the room is completely secure, as the multi-level layout makes you an easy target from above.
</Callout>
