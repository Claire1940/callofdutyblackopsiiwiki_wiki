<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Optimization for High FPS">

If your machine barely meets the **Call of Duty Black Ops II system requirements**, follow these steps to maximize your frame rate and reduce input lag for a smoother competitive experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Adjust In-Game Video Settings">
    Go to Options > Video. Set 'Texture Quality' to Medium and 'Shadows' to Low. Disable 'Depth of Field' and 'Ambient Occlusion' as these are the most resource-heavy settings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Frame Rate Limits">
    Set 'Max Frames Per Second' to match your monitor's refresh rate (e.g., 60, 144, or 240). Avoid using 'Unlimited' if your CPU is weak, as it can cause thermal throttling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Update Graphics Drivers">
    Even though the game is older, modern drivers from Nvidia and AMD include legacy optimizations that can fix flickering textures or crashes on Windows 11.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Configure Steam Launch Options">
    Right-click the game in Steam > Properties. Under 'Launch Options', you can add specific commands to force the game to run in high priority or windowed borderless mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>
