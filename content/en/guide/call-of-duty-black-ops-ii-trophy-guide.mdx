<Section icon={Trophy} title="Campaign Milestones & Strike Force Operations">

Achieving the Platinum trophy in Call of Duty: Black Ops II requires a mix of tactical precision, campaign mastery, and cooperative survival. The campaign features branching storylines and Strike Force missions that directly impact the narrative outcome and your trophy progress. Unlike traditional linear campaigns, this entry introduces Sandbox elements that demand strategic planning.

One of the most notable Strike Force achievements is **Desert Storm**. In this mission, players must secure a convoy moving through hostile territory, utilizing both ground forces and remote-controlled machinery. Success depends on how efficiently you toggle between the tactical overhead view and direct control of assets.

**Video Highlights:**
- **Convoy Protection**: Guard vehicles Alpha, Bravo, Charlie, and Delta from RPGs.
- **Drone Deployment**: Use Quad Drones and Autonomous Security Drones (ASDs) for defense.
- **Cavalry Defense**: Intercept enemy cavalry on horseback before they plant IEDs.
- **Air Superiority**: Direct Quad Drones to neutralize incoming enemy helicopters.

To secure the Desert Storm achievement, players must guide the convoy safely to the extraction zone. The primary threat comes from RPG units positioned on the surrounding ridges and enemy armor moving in from the front. Switch to your ASDs to absorb damage and return heavy firepower, while using Quad Drones to clear out infantry on the hilltops.

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Trophy Roadmap">

To optimize your playtime, follow a structured approach to the campaign, multiplayer, and Zombies modes. This minimizes the need for multiple full playthroughs and ensures you do not miss time-sensitive trophies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Veteran Campaign Playthrough">
    Begin the campaign on Veteran difficulty. This unlocks both the difficulty-related trophies and allows you to familiarize yourself with the branching story paths. Focus on making decisions that keep key characters alive, as this is required for specific narrative trophies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Challenge Clean-up and Intel">
    Replay missions via the Mission Select menu on Recruit difficulty to complete the remaining campaign challenges and collect all Intel items. Completing all 100 challenges is the most time-consuming part of the single-player experience.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strike Force Mastery">
    Complete all Strike Force missions. These missions are easier to manage when you take direct control of the ASDs and Quad Drones rather than relying solely on the AI commander mode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Zombies and Multiplayer Achievements">
    Coordinate with a dedicated group of players to complete the Zombies Easter Eggs, particularly the Tower of Babble trophy. Finish the remaining multiplayer trophies by participating in public matches and leveling up your rank.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Zombies Mode Master Strategies">

The Zombies mode in this installment features some of the most complex cooperative trophies in the series. The centerpiece is the **Tower of Babble** trophy, which requires players to obey the voices in Green Run (Tranzit). This Easter Egg can be completed via two different paths: Maxis's path or Richtofen's path.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Maxis's Path">
    Requires players to turn off the power, build the Jet Gun, and lure the electric zombie under the pylon while keeping it powered down.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Richtofen's Path">
    Requires keeping the power on, building the Jet Gun, heating up the obelisk, and EMPing specific streetlights around the map.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Survival Setup">
    Prioritize building the Turbine and Zombie Shield early to protect your back while navigating the fog between locations.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Successfully navigating Tranzit requires careful resource management. You must manage the bus schedule, avoid the Denizens in the fog, and build key buildable items at workbenches scattered across the map.

| Zombies Trophy | Map | Key Requirement | Players Needed |
|-|-|-|-|
| **Tower of Babble** | Green Run | Complete Richtofen or Maxis side of the Easter Egg | 2 to 4 Players |
| **Don't Fire Until You See** | Green Run | Open all doors in Tranzit without setting on fire | 1 to 4 Players |
| **The Lights Of Their Eyes** | Green Run | Pacify a Denizen using a streetlight | 1 Player |
| **Undead Man's Party Bus** | Green Run | Complete all modifications to the bus in one game | 1 to 4 Players |
| **Dance on My Grave** | Green Run | Acquire your Tombstone perk drop | 1 Player |

<Callout type="info" title="EMP Grenade Utility">
EMP Grenades are crucial for both the Richtofen Easter Egg path and for temporarily disabling the electric zombie (Avogadro). Keep spinning the Mystery Box until at least one player on your team secures them.
</Callout>
