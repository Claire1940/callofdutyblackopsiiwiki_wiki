<Section icon={Shield} title="Historical Figures vs. Fictional Antagonists">

The **call of duty black ops ii timeline** is praised for its integration of real-world history. The developers at Treyarch consulted with experts like Oliver North to ground the 1980s segments in reality. This creates a stark contrast between the grounded military history and the speculative fiction of 2025.

**Jonas Savimbi and UNITA:**
The game accurately depicts Savimbi as a charismatic and fierce leader of the anti-communist UNITA movement. In 2026, his portrayal remains a point of historical discussion, reflecting the complex nature of Cold War proxy fighters who were supported by the US to counter Soviet influence in Africa.

**Manuel Noriega and the Panama Invasion:**
Noriega’s transition from a CIA asset to an "enemy of the state" is a central theme in the 1989 missions. The game recreates the tension of Operation Just Cause, including the psychological warfare used by US forces.
