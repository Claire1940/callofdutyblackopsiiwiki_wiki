### Cove: Tropical Survival
Cove is a small, circular map set on a remote island in the Indian Ocean. The center of the map is dominated by a crashed airplane, which serves as the primary chokepoint. 
- **Aggressive Play**: Use SMGs like the MSMC to weave between the rocky outcroppings on the shoreline.
- **Defense**: Hold the cockpit of the aircraft to watch the two main paths leading from the beach.

### Detour: Bridge Warfare
Set on a destroyed suspension bridge in the United States, Detour is a long, narrow map with two distinct levels.
- **Upper Level**: Favors Assault Rifles and Snipers. Use vehicle husks for cover.
- **Lower Level**: A series of maintenance walkways perfect for flanking and objective rotations in Search and Destroy.

### Rush: Paintball Chaos
Rush is arguably the most popular map in the Vengeance pack. It mimics a competitive paintball arena with high-visibility obstacles and very fast engagement times.
- **Movement**: Stay mobile. The "Speedball" lane allows for extremely fast spawntrapping if your team coordinates.
- **Equipment**: Trophy Systems are vital here to block the constant barrage of Concussion grenades.

### Uplink: The Return of Summit
Uplink is a futuristic reimagining of Summit. It retains the classic three-lane structure but updates the aesthetic to match the 2025 setting of Black Ops II.
- **Central Control**: Controlling the main interior room is the key to winning Domination matches.
- **Cliffside Path**: Use this for stealthy flanks, but be careful not to fall off the edge during high-intensity gunfights.

<Section icon={Target} title="Buried Zombies: The Ultimate Guide">

The crown jewel of **call of duty black ops ii vengeance** is undoubtedly "Buried." Set in a collapsed underground Old West town, this map introduced unique mechanics that shifted the Zombies meta toward heavy resource management and cooperation with a friendly NPC.

### Working with Arthur (The Giant)
Arthur is a massive NPC found in a jail cell near the beginning of the map. By feeding him Booze or Candy, you can manipulate his behavior to assist your team.
- **Booze**: Arthur charges forward, breaking barricades or destroying the fountain.
- **Candy**: Arthur performs context-sensitive actions, such as building equipment, holding a crawler, or protecting a specific area.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Free Arthur">
    Locate the key in the Courthouse or near the jail cell and open Arthur's door.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Booze/Candy">
    Booze spawns in the Saloon or the Jail. Candy spawns in the Candy Shop. Feed these to Arthur to trigger his abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build the Subsurface Resonator">
    Gather parts from the General Store and have Arthur build the resonator to create a powerful sonic defense.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate the Mansion">
    Pass through the haunted mansion (avoiding the ghosts who steal your points) to reach the Maze and Pack-a-Punch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### The Paralyzer Wonder Weapon
The Paralyzer is a unique wonder weapon that fires a continuous beam of energy. It has infinite ammo but operates on a cooling mechanic.
- **Combat**: It slows and eventually disintegrates Zombies.
- **Utility**: Aiming the Paralyzer at the ground and jumping allows the player to "fly" or hover, reaching rooftops and skipping parkour sections.

| Buildable Item | Location of Parts | Primary Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trample Steam** | General Store | Flings players and zombies across gaps |
| **Head Chopper** | General Store | Automated blade trap for doorways |
| **Turbine** | General Store | Powers other buildables and doors |
| **Subsurface Resonator** | General Store | High-damage sonic wave (requires Turbine) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Ghost Encounter">
When entering the Mansion, ghosts will spawn and touch you, stealing 2,000 points per hit. If you have no points, they will steal a Perk. Ensure your team moves quickly or uses the Paralyzer to clear them out.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Ray Gun Mark II and Weapon Meta">

The **call of duty black ops ii vengeance** DLC changed the weapon landscape by introducing the Ray Gun Mark II. Unlike the original Ray Gun, which dealt splash damage and often downed the user in close quarters, the Mark II is a precision instrument.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ray Gun Mark II Pros">
    - **No Splash Damage**: Safe to use at point-blank range.
    - **High Penetration**: Can hit multiple zombies in a line.
    - **Burst Fire**: High accuracy and ammo conservation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ray Gun Mark II Cons">
    - **Requires Aim**: Must hit headshots for maximum efficiency.
    - **Burst Delay**: Small gap between shots can be dangerous.
    - **Mystery Box Only**: Rare drop rate compared to wall weapons.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

In multiplayer, the meta during the Vengeance season often revolves around high-mobility builds to handle the verticality of maps like Detour and the tight corners of Rush. 

**Recommended Loadout for Rush:**
- **Primary**: MSMC with Fast Mag and Laser Sight.
- **Perks**: Lightweight, Toughness, Dexterity.
- **Wildcard**: Perk 3 Greed (add Extreme Conditioning).
- **Tactical**: Trophy System (essential for objective play).

<Callout type="success" title="Weapon Tip">
The Ray Gun Mark II is incredibly effective against the "Big" zombies in other maps, such as the Brutus on Mob of the Dead or the Panzer Soldat on Origins. If you are playing those maps, having a teammate with Vengeance owned is a huge advantage.
</Callout>
