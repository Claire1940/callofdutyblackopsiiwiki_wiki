<Section icon={Target} title="Multiplayer Map Breakdown & Strategies">

The **call of duty black ops ii revolution dlc** delivers four distinct multiplayer maps set across diverse global environments. Each map rewards specific playstyles, from tight close-quarters combat to long-range tactical sniping.

### Hydro
Set along the Indus River in Pakistan, Hydro is a medium-sized map defined by its central floodway.

| Lanes | Primary Combat Style | Map Hazard |
|--- |--- |--- |
| Three-lane layout | Mid-to-long range gunfights | Periodic flooding that instantly kills players in the center |

### Downhill
Located in the snowy Rhone-Alps of France, Downhill features open outdoor spaces and a functional ski lodge.

| Key Vantage Points | Recommended Weapon Class | Interactive Element |
|--- |--- |--- |
| Cabin balconies, sniper cliffs | Assault Rifles, Sniper Rifles | Moving gondolas that provide cover or act as lethal obstacles |

### Grind
Set in a vibrant skate park in Venice Beach, California, Grind is a fan-favorite map that emphasizes fluid movement.

| Area | Combat Type | Top Strategy |
|--- |--- |--- |
| Skate bowls & half-pipes | Fast-paced, close-quarters | Use Shotguns/SMGs to clear the curved, indoor structures |
| Outer boardwalk | Long-range lanes | Hold angles with Assault Rifles to cut off rotating players |

### Mirage
Set in China's Gobi Desert, Mirage is a luxury resort devastated by a massive sandstorm.

| Layout Characteristics | Flanking Routes | Defensive Tips |
|--- |--- |--- |
| Circular center, sand dunes | Climb dunes to access the hotel's second floor | Watch the open courtyard; cover is sparse due to sand buildup |

<Callout type="info" title="Grind Trivia">
The aesthetic of Grind was heavily influenced by Tony Hawk's Pro Skater HD, another popular title published by Activision.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Die Rise & Turned: Zombies Expansion">

The Revolution DLC brings a massive update for Zombies fans, introducing the vertical playground of **Die Rise** and the frantic PvP/PvE hybrid mode, **Turned**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Navigate the Skyscrapers">
    Die Rise is set in two crumbling skyscrapers in Shanghai, China. Watch your step, as falling off the edge results in instant death. Use the elevators to move between floors and access key perks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble the Buildables">
    Search both towers for parts to construct the Navcard Table and the Sliquifier, a powerful Wonder Weapon that shoots slippery, chain-reacting goo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Turned Mode">
    In Turned, three players act as zombies hunting a single human survivor. The human uses a reverse gun game progression, fighting to stay alive while zombies try to land a single melee strike to swap roles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Achievement Name | Description | Gamerscore / Trophy |

| High Maintenance | Be the architect of Maxis or Richtofen's instruction in Die Rise. | 75G / Silver |
