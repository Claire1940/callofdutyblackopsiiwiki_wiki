<Section icon={Swords} title="Multiplayer Maps: Tactical Breakdown">

The Revolution DLC adds four distinct Multiplayer maps that cater to various playstyles, from the snowy slopes of the Alps to a vibrant skate park in California. Each map introduces unique environmental hazards or movement opportunities that require players to adjust their Pick 10 loadouts accordingly.

### The Four Core Arenas
1. **Downhill**: Set in the French Alps, this medium-sized map features a ski lodge and moving gondolas. The gondolas act as both cover and a lethal hazard; getting hit by one results in an instant death.
2. **Hydro**: Located at a hydroelectric dam in Pakistan, this map is centered around a spillway. Periodically, the dam will release water, flooding the central lane and killing anyone caught in the path.
3. **Mirage**: A luxury resort in the Gobi Desert devastated by a sandstorm. The dunes have created natural ramps and vertical sightlines, making it a haven for snipers and long-range assault rifles.
4. **Grind**: A colorful skate park in Venice Beach. The curved ramps and bowls provide unusual cover angles, favoring fast-moving SMG players who can navigate the tight corners.

| Map Name | Combat Profile | Key Hazard / Feature | Recommended Weapon |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Downhill** | Long sightlines, lodge interiors | Moving gondolas (Lethal) | Sniper / Assault Rifle |
| **Hydro** | Three-lane, industrial | Periodic flooding (Lethal) | SMG / Shotgun |
| **Mirage** | Open dunes, interior hotel | Sand-covered verticality | Assault Rifle / LMG |
| **Grind** | Close-quarters, curved cover | Skate park bowls/ramps | SMG / Peacekeeper |

<Callout type="tip" title="Grind Strategy">
On Grind, use the curved bowls to "head-glitch." By standing just behind the lip of a ramp, you can see oncoming enemies while only exposing a small portion of your character's head, making you a difficult target for anyone not using a precision weapon.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Die Rise Zombies: Navigating the Great Leap Forward">

Die Rise, the Zombies map included in **call of duty black ops ii revolution**, takes place in a pair of crumbling skyscrapers in a ruined Chinese city. This map is famous (and sometimes infamous) for its verticality and the use of elevators to transport players and machines between floors. Unlike the sprawling fog of TranZit, Die Rise focuses on narrow corridors and dangerous jumps.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Restoring the Power">
    From the spawn room, you must navigate down several floors to reach the Power Station. Turning on the power is essential as it activates the elevators, which carry Perk-a-Cola machines and the Pack-a-Punch machine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Building the Sliquifier">
    The Sliquifier is the map's Wonder Weapon. You need to collect four parts (Mannequin Foot, Disc, Handle, and Pressure Cooker) found around the power room and the floors above it. This weapon fires a slippery purple goo that makes zombies fall and chain-explode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mastering the Elevators">
    Perk machines in Die Rise are located inside elevators. They move randomly between floors. Always check the top of the elevator car before stepping in; if the machine isn't on that specific car, you might get trapped or crushed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Trample Steam">
    This buildable equipment is vital for crossing large gaps between buildings. Place it at the edge of a roof to launch yourself (or zombies) across the chasm to the adjacent skyscraper.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Falling Hazard">
Die Rise is the only map where "falling to your death" is a constant threat. Be extremely careful when jumping between buildings or navigating the elevator shafts. Always ensure you have a clear landing zone before committing to a jump.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Peacekeeper SMG: The Hybrid Meta">

The Peacekeeper SMG is a unique addition to the **call of duty black ops ii revolution** pack. It is classified as an SMG, meaning you maintain high movement speed and fast Aim-Down-Sights (ADS) times, but it possesses the range and accuracy of an Assault Rifle. This makes it a dominant choice for medium-range maps like Mirage or Downhill.

### The "Turned" Mode Explained
Revolution introduced a new way to play Zombies: **Turned**. In this mode, one player starts as the Human, and three others are Zombies. The goal for the Zombies is to kill the Human. The player who delivers the finishing blow becomes the Human and earns points by surviving and killing the AI-controlled and player-controlled zombies. The player with the most points at the end of the time limit wins.

| Mechanic | Human Player | Zombie Players |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Goal** | Survive and earn points | Kill the Human to swap roles |
| **Weapons** | Randomly cycling guns | Melee only |
| **Perks** | Instant reload / healing | Increased speed / lunging |
| **Score** | Earned per second alive | Earned by killing Human |

<Callout type="info" title="Turned Strategy">
As a Zombie, try to corner the Human. If all three player-controlled zombies attack from different angles simultaneously, the Human will be overwhelmed by the reload time of their current weapon, making a role swap inevitable.
</Callout>
