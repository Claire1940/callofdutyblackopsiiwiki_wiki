The crown jewel of the **call of duty black ops ii uprising** DLC is undoubtedly **Mob of the Dead**. <Section icon={Target} title="How to Build the Plane and Pack-a-Punch">

Escaping Alcatraz is a multi-step cooperative puzzle that requires precise coordination. Players must gather five distinct parts scattered across the prison, build the Icarus escape plane on the roof, and fly to the Golden Gate Bridge to access the Pack-a-Punch machine.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Warden's Key">
    Locate the blue glowing key hanging near the Cafeteria or the Warden's Office. Enter the Afterlife state nearby, shock the electrical box to lower the key, and grab it once you revive yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect the Five Escape Parts">
    Use the Warden's Key and the Afterlife state to unlock the laundry door, the rigging in the spiral spiral staircase, the oxygen tank in the infirmary, the engine valve in the docks, and the control unit in the generator room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assemble and Launch the Plane">
    Carry all five parts to the prison roof. Interact with the wooden ramp to assemble the Icarus plane. Once fully built, all players must board the plane together to trigger the flight to the Golden Gate Bridge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade at the Bridge">
    Upon landing at the bridge, locate the Pack-a-Punch machine at the far end. Survive the endless wave of zombies on the bridge to upgrade your weapons for 5,000 points. Use the electric chairs to return to the prison when finished.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Golden Gate Bridge Pro-Tip">
No perk machines are available on the bridge, and your escape plane will not return until you take the electric chairs back to Alcatraz and recharge the fuel canisters. Plan your upgrades carefully before flying out.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Multiplayer Map Strategy and Lane Control">

The multiplayer component of the **call of duty black ops ii uprising** expansion features four diverse maps designed to accommodate various playstyles. From tight, chaotic close-quarters engagements to long-range sniper duels, mastering these layouts is essential for dominating public lobbies and League Play matches.

| Map Name | Combat Profile | Dominant Lane/Route | Recommended Weapon Class |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Studio** | Fast-paced, symmetrical three-lane arena | Central trailer park and elevated tower | SMGs (MSMC) or Assault Rifles (M8A1) |
| **Magma** | Hazard-based lane control with active lava | Central bridge and flanking subway station | Assault Rifles (AN-94) or LMGs (LSAT) |
| **Encore** | Circular flow with a highly exposed center | Main performance stage and outer curved walkways | SMGs (PDW-57) or Shotguns (R870 MCS) |
| **Vertigo** | Vertical high-rise with high-risk drop zones | Central laboratory and lower maintenance paths | Sniper Rifles (DSR 50) or Tactical Rifles |

<Callout type="info" title="Lava Hazards on Magma">
The glowing lava pools on Magma are instantly lethal. You cannot slide or jump over wide lava cracks. Use these hazards to your advantage by trapping enemies against the lava flows or locking down the safe bridges with tactical equipment like Trophy Systems.
</Callout>
