<Section icon={Target} title="Origins Zombies: Main Objectives & Setup">

Origins is widely considered one of the most complex and rewarding Zombies maps in Call of Duty history. Set in a dieselpunk-themed World War I battlefield, it introduces giant patrolling robots, the deadly Panzer Soldat, and the original four heroes: Dempsey, Nikolai, Takeo, and Richtofen.

To establish a successful run on Origins, players must prioritize powering the map, building the elemental weapons, and preparing for the arrival of the Panzer Soldat on Round 8.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Power the Generators">
    Activate all six Steam Generators scattered across the map. Each generator costs points to activate and spawns aggressive Templar Zombies that attempt to disrupt the process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble Elemental Staffs">
    Collect the three hidden parts, the corresponding colored record, and the elemental crystal for the Ice, Fire, Wind, or Lightning Staff. Access the Crazy Place via the elemental tunnels to forge them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Pack-a-Punch">
    Once all six generators are simultaneously active, the Pack-a-Punch machine at the top of the Excavation Site will open. Use this machine to upgrade your weapons for 5,000 points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Panzer Soldat">
    On Round 8, the armored Panzer Soldat spawns. Target his glowing red power core or his helmet with high-damage weapons like the Ray Gun Mark II or an upgraded wall weapon to eliminate him quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Generator Locations">
- **Generator 1**: Spawn Room
- **Generator 2**: Trenches (near the Tank Station)
- **Generator 3**: Fire Tunnel area
- **Generator 4**: Wind Tunnel area (near Jugger-Nog)
- **Generator 5**: Lightning Tunnel area (near Stamin-Up)
- **Generator 6**: Church (behind the excavation site)
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Elemental Staff Guide">

The four Elemental Staffs are the ultimate wonder weapons of Origins. Each staff features a unique elemental attack and can be upgraded into an incredibly powerful ancient variant.

| Staff | How to Find Parts | Upgrade Puzzle Theme | Combat Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ice Staff** | Dig up glowing dirt piles during active snow rounds | Match blue tile symbols in the Crazy Place | Freezes and shatters groups of zombies |
| **Fire Staff** | Shoot down the glowing plane, power Generator 6, defeat the Panzer | Decode glowing torch sequences in the Church | Spawns lava pools that melt targets |
| **Wind Staff** | Shoot the glowing yellow feet of the three Giant Robots and enter them | Align rotating rings in the ceiling of the Crazy Place | Blasts enemies away with a powerful tornado |
| **Lightning Staff** | Ride the tank and jump off onto elevated wooden platforms | Play specific musical notes on the Crazy Place keyboard | Chain-lightning shock that paralyzes targets |

<Callout type="success" title="Upgrade Tip">
The Ice Staff is the easiest to build early in solo games, while its upgraded form, Ull's Arrow, offers the most effective crowd control during high-round survival.
</Callout>
