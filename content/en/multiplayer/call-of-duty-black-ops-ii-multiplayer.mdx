### Competitive Rotations
In modes like Hardpoint, the objective moves every 60 seconds. High-level players begin rotating to the next location when the current timer hits 15 seconds. This "pre-rotation" allows your team to set up defenses and control the spawns before the enemy even arrives at the new Hill.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Progression and Scorestreak Rewards">

Success in **call of duty black ops ii multiplayer** isn't just about kills; it's about earning your Scorestreaks. Unlike traditional Killstreaks, Scorestreaks accumulate points from kills, assists, and objective captures. This system encourages players to play the "PTFO" (Play The F***ing Objective) style.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Streak Tier">
    Choose streaks you can realistically earn. Beginners should stick to UAV, Hunter Killer, and Care Package. Advanced players can aim for the VSAT and K9 Unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Objective Integration">
    In Domination, a kill while standing on a flag rewards nearly double the score of a standard kill. Use this to jump-start your streak progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The VSAT Peak">
    The Orbital VSAT is the most powerful streak in the game because it cannot be shot down and shows enemy real-time movement. Once a VSAT is up, your team is almost guaranteed to earn their higher-tier rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Scorestreak Tip">
If you are struggling to reach high streaks, equip the Hardline perk. It reduces the score required for all streaks by 20%, making the jump from a UAV to a Lightning Strike much easier.
</Callout>

</Section>
