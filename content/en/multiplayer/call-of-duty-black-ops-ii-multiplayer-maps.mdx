### Key Base Map Highlights

Each map requires a distinct tactical approach. For instance, **Raid** features a highly symmetrical design that makes it a favorite for competitive search and destroy modes. Players must fight for control of the central courtyard and the ring area to secure clean lines of sight into the enemy spawns.

In contrast, **Turbine** offers a massive, open desert landscape. Here, assault rifles and sniper rifles dominate the long sightlines. Players must navigate the high ground around the fallen wind turbines while avoiding the exposed lower valley.

**Hijacked** remains one of the most popular close-quarters combat arenas. The layout forces constant action, with the central cabin serving as a highly contested zone. Successful players often utilize the lower engine room deck to flank enemies who are focused on the upper decks.

In competitive play, map control is everything. Teams must work together to hold spawns, secure choke points, and anchor objective locations. Maps like Raid, Standoff, and Slums form the backbone of competitive modes such as Hardpoint and Search and Destroy.
