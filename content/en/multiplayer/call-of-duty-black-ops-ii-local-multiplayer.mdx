<Section icon={Settings} title="How to Start a Local Split-Screen Match">

Starting a session for **call of duty black ops ii local multiplayer** is straightforward, but ensuring both players are correctly recognized by the system is key to avoiding lobby glitches.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Main Menu">
    Launch the game and select "Multiplayer" from the primary mode selection screen (Campaign, Multiplayer, Zombies).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Local Mode">
    Navigate down to the "Local" or "System Link" option. For a single console with two controllers, "Local" is the correct choice.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Connect the Second Controller">
    Once in the local lobby, turn on the second controller. On PlayStation, assign it to a guest or secondary profile. On Xbox, sign in to a secondary account.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Join the Lobby">
    Press the "X" (PlayStation) or "A" (Xbox) button on the second controller. The second player's name should appear in the lobby list below the host.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Configure Game Settings">
    The host can now select "Setup Game" to choose the map, mode, and custom rules like health, time limits, and bot difficulty.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Bot Integration">
If you only have two human players, use the "General" settings in the "Setup Game" menu to add AI Bots. You can add up to 9 bots to fill out a 6v6 Team Deathmatch experience locally.
</Callout>
