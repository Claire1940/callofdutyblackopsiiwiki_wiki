<Section icon={Target} title="Understanding the Scorestreak Evolution">

The transition to the Scorestreak system represents one of the most significant mechanical shifts in the franchise. Unlike traditional titles where only kills progressed your rewards, **call of duty black ops ii killstreaks** reward comprehensive team play. This system incentivizes players to capture points, defend positions, and provide support via assists.

For instance, while a standard kill grants 100 points, contributing to a team goal like capturing a flag provides 200 points. Furthermore, active support streaks like the UAV provide "assist points" every time a teammate secures a kill while your radar is active. This creates a feedback loop where support players can reach high-tier rewards through utility rather than pure combat prowess.

| Action Type | Point Reward | Tactical Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Standard Kill | 100 Points | Basic progression toward rewards |
| Flag Capture | 200 Points | High-value objective play |
| UAV Assist | 10 Points | Passive income while active |
| Destroying Equipment | 50-100 Points | Denies enemy utility while gaining points |
| Major Assist | 50 Points | Rewards teamwork and damage dealing |

<Callout type="tip" title="Objective Efficiency">
