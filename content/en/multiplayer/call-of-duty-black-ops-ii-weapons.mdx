<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Weapon Mechanics and the Pick 10 System">

Understanding how **call of duty black ops ii weapons** function starts with the revolutionary Pick 10 system. Unlike previous titles, this game allows you to allocate exactly ten points across your primary weapon, secondary weapon, attachments, perks, lethal equipment, tactical equipment, and wildcards. This flexibility means you can sacrifice a sidearm to stack three attachments on your assault rifle or drop all equipment to run six perks.

In the 2026 PS4 and PS5 release, the weapon handling and hit detection remain faithful to the original experience. This means that recoil patterns, fire rates, and damage drop-off values are consistent with the classic meta. Mastery of these mechanics involves learning the "burst" nature of certain rifles and the "rushing" potential of high-mobility submachine guns.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Competitive Rifle and SMG Comparisons">

Creating a dominant loadout with **call of duty black ops ii weapons** requires more than just picking a strong gun; you must synergize your perks and attachments to match your intended role. The following steps outline how to construct a professional-grade class using the Pick 10 system.

  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Primary Role">

  <Step num="2" title="Equip Essential Attachments">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance Your Perks">
    **Toughness** (Perk 2) is non-negotiable for competitive play as it reduces flinch when shot. **Flak Jacket** (Perk 1) is essential for objective modes to survive grenades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalize with Equipment">
    Use remaining points on **EMP Grenades** to disable enemy Sentry Guns and Guardians, or a **C4** for defending objectives.
