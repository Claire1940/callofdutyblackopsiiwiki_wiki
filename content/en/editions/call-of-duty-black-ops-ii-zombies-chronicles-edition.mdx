<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Early Round Setup Guide">

To achieve success on Origins within the **call of duty black ops ii zombies chronicles edition** environment, you must execute a highly optimized early-round route. This sequence ensures you maximize points, activate power, and begin crafting your staffs before the difficulty scales up.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Spawn Room Optimization">
    Do not buy any wall weapons in the spawn room. Use your starting pistol to shoot zombies in the leg 5-6 times before finishing them with a melee strike. This maximizes your point yield. Activate Generator 1 before leaving the room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trench Progression">
    Open the door leading toward Generator 2 or Generator 3. Grab the shovel spawning in these areas. Dig up glowing piles of dirt immediately, as they can yield extra points, weapons, or vital staff parts during snow rounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="No Man's Land and Shield Parts">
    Clear the path to the center of the map (No Man's Land). Your priority here is to locate the three shield parts scattered around the generator areas and the trenches. Build the zombie shield at the workspace under the excavation site to protect your back.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Staff Assembly and Upgrades">
    Begin gathering the pieces for your chosen elemental staff. The Ice Staff is typically the easiest to build early, as its parts are dug up from the ground during active snow rounds. Once assembled, take the staff to the Crazy Place to begin the upgrade process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Generator Defilers">
Randomly throughout your run, Templar Zombies will teleport in to attack active generators. If they succeed, they will disable the generator and steal your Pack-a-Punch access. Defend the generators quickly to secure a free Max Ammo power-up.
</Callout>

</Section>
