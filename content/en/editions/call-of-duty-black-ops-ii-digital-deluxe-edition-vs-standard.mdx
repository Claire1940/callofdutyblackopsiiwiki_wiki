<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Setting Up Your First Custom Class">

Once you have selected your edition, you need to set up your multiplayer profile. Custom classes unlock at Level 4. Use these steps to build a highly competitive class using the **Pick 10 system**, which allocates exactly ten points across weapons, attachments, perks, and equipment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select a Primary Weapon">
    Choose a versatile weapon like the **AN-94** (S-Tier Assault Rifle) or the **MSMC** (S-Tier SMG). If you purchased the Digital Deluxe Edition, you can also select the **Peacekeeper SMG** immediately. This consumes **1 Point**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Essential Attachments">
    Add a **Quickdraw Handle** to reduce aim-down-sights time and a **Fast Mag** or **Stock** to maintain movement speed. Each attachment consumes **1 Point**. To equip three attachments, you must use the **Primary Gunfighter Wildcard** (costs 1 extra point).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assign Survival Perks">
    Equip **Flak Jacket** (Perk 1) to survive explosive spam, **Toughness** (Perk 2) to reduce flinch when shot, and **Dexterity** (Perk 3) to aim faster after sprinting. These three perks consume **3 Points** and are essential for winning head-on gunfights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Fill Lethal and Tactical Slots">
    Equip a **Semtex Grenade** for quick explosive damage and a **Trophy System** or **EMP Grenade** to disable hostile electronic equipment. These consume your remaining points to hit the maximum **Pick 10 limit**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Ideal Beginner Class Setup">
If you are new to the game, try combining the **MTAR Assault Rifle** with a **Reflex Sight**, **Flak Jacket**, **Toughness**, and **Dexterity**. This build offers low recoil, clear visibility, and high defensive protection.
