<Section icon={Target} title="How to Build Your First Class: Step-by-Step">

Creating an efficient loadout is the first step toward climbing the multiplayer ranks. Follow these steps to build a balanced, beginner-friendly class once you unlock Create-a-Class at Level 4.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select a Versatile Primary Weapon">
    Choose a highly reliable weapon like the MTAR or MP7. These firearms offer predictable recoil patterns and balanced damage profiles that perform well across close, medium, and long-range engagements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Crucial Handling Attachments">
    Spend your next two points on a Reflex Sight and a Quickdraw Handle. The Reflex Sight provides a clean, unobstructed view of your targets, while the Quickdraw Handle significantly decreases your aim-down-sights (ADS) time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Allocate Points to Survival Perks">
    Equip Flak Jacket (Perk 1) to survive explosive spam, Toughness (Perk 2) to reduce flinch when shot, and Dexterity (Perk 3) to aim faster after sprinting. These three perks form the absolute foundation of competitive survival.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Add Lethal and Tactical Utility">
    Fill your remaining slots with a Semtex grenade for reliable explosive damage and a Concussion Grenade to disorient defenders before pushing high-traffic objective zones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Low-Cost Scorestreaks">
As a beginner, equip low-cost Scorestreaks like the UAV, Hunter Killer Drone, and Care Package. These are much easier to earn consistently and provide massive utility to your entire team.
</Callout>

</Section>
