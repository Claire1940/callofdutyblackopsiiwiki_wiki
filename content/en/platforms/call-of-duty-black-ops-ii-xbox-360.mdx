Playing **call of duty black ops ii xbox 360** remains one of the most popular ways to experience this classic near-future military shooter. <Section icon={Map} title="How to Setup and Play on Modern Xbox Consoles">

Transitioning your campaign progression, multiplayer ranks, and downloadable content to modern Xbox platforms is straightforward. Because the game utilizes cloud storage for Xbox 360 profiles, your legacy multiplayer progress carries over automatically as long as you log in with the same Xbox credentials.

Follow these steps to set up the game on your Xbox One or Xbox Series X|S console:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Insert Disc or Locate Digital License">
    Insert your physical Xbox 360 disc into your Xbox One or Xbox Series X console to prompt the automatic download. If you own the digital version, go to **My Games & Apps** > **Full Library** > **Owned Games** and select the game from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Download the Compatibility Package">
    Allow the console to download the packaged version of the game. Modern consoles do not play the game directly off the physical disc; instead, the disc acts as a security key while the system downloads a compatible digital version (~16 GB).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sync Your Xbox 360 Profile and Cloud Saves">
    Launch the game. Press both the **View** and **Menu** buttons (the two small buttons in the center of your controller) simultaneously to open the Xbox 360 guide overlay. Log in to your profile and enable cloud saves to download your legacy campaign progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Online Requirements">
    Ensure your console has an active internet connection. Online multiplayer and co-op Zombies matchmaking require an active **Xbox Game Pass Core** or **Xbox Game Pass Ultimate** subscription. Single-player local modes can be accessed offline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="DLC Installation Check">
If you previously purchased the Season Pass or individual DLC packs on your Xbox 360, manage the game installation from your modern dashboard. Press the **Menu** button on the game icon, select **Manage game & add-ons**, and check the boxes next to your owned map packs to download them.
</Callout>

</Section>
